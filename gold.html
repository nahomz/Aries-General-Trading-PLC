Overview of Gold Exports from Ethiopia

Ethiopia is rich in mineral resources, with gold being one of the most significant export commodities. The country has seen fluctuations in gold production and export levels, with a reported production of approximately 3,480 kg in December 2022, which remained stable from the previous year. Gold exports play a crucial role in Ethiopia's economy, contributing significantly to foreign exchange earnings.


Gold Production and Export Challenges

Despite its potential, Ethiopia's gold export sector faces challenges, including issues related to smuggling. A significant volume of gold extracted in the country is reportedly being smuggled out, which undermines official export figures and revenue. The government has been working to combat this issue and improve the regulation of the gold mining sector.
