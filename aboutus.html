About

Aries General Trading PLC

Based in the vibrant city of Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, Aries General Trading P.L.C is a pioneering company specializing in gold mining and the agricultural sector.

Since our establishment in 2018, we have been committed to sustainable practices and responsible resource management, positioning ourselves as a leader in these essential industries. Our operations are supported by a comprehensive licensing framework, enabling us to engage in diverse activities, including recycling, wholesale trade, and the export of agricultural products.



Mining Sector

Agriculture Sector

Agricultural Exports: From our base in Ethiopia, Aries General Trading is dedicated to promoting agricultural products on a global scale. We export a diverse range of commodities, including fruits, vegetables, oilseeds, and pulses, ensuring they meet international quality standards to provide our clients with premium produce. Animal Products and Live Animals: We also specialize in the export of live animals and animal products, adhering strictly to regulatory requirements to ensure the health and safety of our livestock and products.

Gold Mining Operations: In the mining sector, our primary focus is the efficient and environmentally responsible extraction of gold. Using advanced mining techniques, we contribute significantly to the industry, with gold mining playing a crucial role in the global market, accounting for approximately 74% of the global gold supply.

Recycling of Waste and Scrap: Our commitment to sustainability is exemplified by our active involvement in the recycling of waste and scrap materials. This initiative helps reduce environmental impact and supports the circular economy by reclaiming valuable resources. Our operations are designed to meet the highest standards of sustainability and efficiency, transforming waste materials into reusable products and minimizing landfill usage.

Licensing and Compliance

Operating under a robust licensing framework, Aries General Trading engages in:

1. Recycling of Waste and Scraps: Promoting responsible waste management and resource recovery.
2. Wholesale Trade in Rubber and Plastic Products: Supplying essential materials to various industries.
3. Wholesale Trade in Stationery, Paper, and Paper Products: Catering to educational and corporate needs.
4. Wholesale Trade in Construction Materials: Providing vital materials for infrastructure development.
5. Export of Agricultural Products: Including a wide array of fruits, vegetables, oilseeds, and pulses.
6. Import Trade in Construction Materials, Metal, and Non-Metals: Facilitating supply chains for construction projects.
7. Export Trade in Live Animals and Animal Products: Committed to ethical practices in livestock trade.
8. Export Trade in Minerals and Mineral Production: Contributing to the global supply of essential minerals.
9. Export Trade in Metal and Non-Metal Scrap: Encouraging recycling and resource recovery.
10. Tour Operation Service: Offering unique experiences that highlight our commitment to sustainability and local culture.


Commitment to Sustainability

At Aries General Trading P.L.C, we are deeply committed to sustainable practices in both mining and agriculture. Our operations minimize environmental impact while maximizing resource efficiency. We continuously strive for improvement and innovation, ensuring that we meet the needs of our clients and the communities we serve in Ethiopia and beyond.

Vision

Our vision is to be a globally recognized leader in the mining and agricultural industries, known for our commitment to sustainability and excellence. We aspire to create a thriving ecosystem where natural resources are managed responsibly, agricultural products are celebrated for their quality, and communities are uplifted through our initiatives. By leveraging advanced technologies and sustainable practices, we aim to contribute significantly to Ethiopia's economic development and environmental preservation.

Mission

At Aries General Trading P.L.C, our mission is to lead the gold mining and agricultural sectors in Ethiopia through sustainable practices and innovative solutions. We are dedicated to providing high-quality products while ensuring environmental stewardship and social responsibility. Our commitment extends to empowering local communities, fostering economic growth, and promoting ethical practices in all our operations. :

To make, distribute and sell the finest quality of products and promote business practices that respect the Earth and the Environment.

Regularly evaluate and improve our capabilities by investing in training and thereby responding to changing client needs.

To imbibe, practice & maintain the quality of products at the apex of its purity, originality, contamination free standards at all analytical parameters of its best quality.
